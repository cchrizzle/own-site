About Me

Hi, I'm Chris, welcome to my page!

A little bit about me: I was born and raised in Socal and went to college in San Diego for Economics. I graduated right when COVID hit in June 2020, and worked for 2 years in commercial real estate before deciding to learn programming. I also worked in the service industry for a number of years and love being around people as a result! I am currently working as a freelance software engineer with a focus on helping others.

I love to cook and am always looking for new recipes and dishes to make. Outside of programming, my hobbies include hiking, jiu-jitsu, working out, and some light jogging to stay in shape. I eventually want to get into astronomy too as I love looking into the stars at night!
